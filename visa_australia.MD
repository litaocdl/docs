1. 注册 [immi](https://online.immi.gov.au/lusc/login) 系统,签证申请类型为visit 600

2. 需要的资料
  * 护照
  
  * 身份证 [奇怪，就是需要](https://www.homeaffairs.gov.au/help-text/eplus/Pages/elp-h1810.aspx#China)
